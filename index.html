STUDENT CAPSTONE PROJECT - FOUNDATION SIXTY6

Working in small groups and functioning as a mini-studio, level 4 students will design, develop, and pitch a professional marketing and advertising campaign.

The mission of Foundation Sixty6 is to facilitate improved access to mental healthcare for youth between the ages of 16 and 25 suffering from psychosis, bipolar disorder (BPD), and schizophrenia, specifically within Southwestern Ontario.

EVENT DETAILS


Join us for a showcase featuring the work of our IDP3 and UED students, alongside Spotlight presentations highlighting the The Brothers in Arms project. Experience the talent and dedication of our students as they proudly present their portfolios, showcasing their passion and skills across various disciplines. After the showcase and viewing of portfolios, we'll continue the celebration across the street at the Chef's Table.

Enjoy appetizers and a cash bar while mingling with fellow attendees. It's the perfect opportunity to network, reconnect with old friends, and forge new connections. This event isn't just about admiring talent it's about actively engaging and giving back. Your feedback and support will empower these budding professionals to reach new heights in their careers.


Together, let's celebrate creativity, innovation, and collaboration.
